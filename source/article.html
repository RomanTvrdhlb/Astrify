How we select experts for consultations in App

We carefully select psychics, esoteric specialists, and astrologers to provide you with high-quality and reliable consultations. Our selection process includes several key stages:

  • Practical testing

    The advisor is asked to answer questions or conduct a mini-consultation using his or her abilities. For example, determining hidden information about a person, predicting events, or reading the energy of an object.

  • Work history and customer reviews

    We analyze previous work experience: did he have clients, what kind of feedback did he receive, did he work in well-known esoteric circles.

  • Interview & Energy Check

    We conduct a one-on-one interview to assess communication skills, empathy, and authenticity. Some specialists naturally give off positive energy during the conversation.

  • Trial Period

    We implement a probationary period to evaluate the quality of consultations, client feedback, and overall satisfaction.

    If you have any issues with our psychics or services, feel free to contact our support team!
